Features

What the app does
for your training.

  • Randomized whistle cadence
    Short, normal, long, or variable. Trains true reaction — not pattern recognition.
  • Reaction + clamp, separately
    Diagnose where you're losing — the whistle response, or the rotation to 90°.
  • Early-jump detection
    Catches false starts the eye can't — with visual and audio feedback in the moment.
  • Trend tracking
    Watch your median reaction time drop week over week. Consistency comes next.
Every screen

Six screens you'll
use every session.

Stays out of your way during reps. Gets sharp when you need to review them.

Progress

30-day median, current streak, monthly rep volume, and an activity calendar. Watch yourself drop time, week after week.

New session

Pick rep count, choose whistle variability and audio cues. A few taps and you're training.

Weekly goals

Sessions per week, a target median time, and a maximum early-jump rate. Train against numbers you set yourself.

Session summary

Median total, reaction/clamp/early splits, and a rep-by-rep chart for the set you just finished.

Live session

Rep counter, audio and visual cues. Pause anytime. Stays out of your way during reps.

Home

Your dashboard. Connection status, last-session data, weekly progress ring, personal best, and recent activity.
